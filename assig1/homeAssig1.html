Introduction

Welcome to my Minecraft beginner's guide. This guide aims for players who have never played Minecraft before and would like to give it a try. The quote The best preparation for tomorrow is doing your best today H. Jackson Brown, Jr. gives an example of why starting off by reading a guide can prepare a player for their in-game adventure. In this guide, I will go over what a player should do once they spawn in their newly generated world and give them insight on what they should be expecting to see and do. This will be demonstrated through an overview, visuals of product objectives and step-by-step instructions.
