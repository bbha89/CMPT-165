Building a Workbench

Building a Crafting Table is probably the first thing that comes to mind for veteran gamers. This is a tool used to build almost everything possible in the Minecraft world. By following these steps, one can create their very own Crafting Table.

  1. Collect logs from trees
  2. Using your 2x2 personal crafting area found in your inventory, place your log into the crafting grid to convert it into wooden planks
  3. Following the same procedure, use 4 planks to convert the planks into a crafting table.

Crafting Objects

The next step would be to craft tools such as a wooden pickaxe. This tool will be handy in order to obtain rare ores and cobblestone needed to make a furnace.

  1. Using the Crafting Table, create sticks by placing 1 wooden plank on top of another
  2. place three planks on the top and 2 sticks down the middle

A furnace will be useful to cook meat for food and smelt ores

  1. Mine 8 cobblestone blocks using your wooden pickaxe
  2. Using your Crafting Table, place the cobblestone blocks everywhere except the middle

Building a shelter

A shelter used to avoid dangers lurking at night.

  1. Mine blocks of your preference
  2. Find an area with a flat terrain
  3. Mine coal found in caves
  4. Using the crafting table,place a stick with a coal placed on top to build a torch
  5. Build your house and lit the area with torches to avoid monster spawning in dark areas

Staying Alive

Food is necessary to survive in the game to keep your health full when facing monsters.

  1. Find animals on terrain
  2. Kill to get raw meat drops
  3. using your furnace, place the meat in the top slot and a coal in the bottom to create cooked meat to eat
