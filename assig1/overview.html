Day Time Objectives

As the first day begins, a player will need to collect resources in order to survive. The main objective for a player when they first spawn inside a world is to collect logs. Logs can be obtained from trees and can be added to a players inventory through holding down the left mouse button while the cursor is on the block. Logs are a neccessary ingredient for players to craft basic tools and materials needed to progress further into the game.

Through the e hotkey, players can access their GUI. Here a player is able to see their inventory where all of their items are stored. Moreover, they will have access to the player's armor slots, recipe book and a 2x2 square crafting area. By utilizing the crafting area, a player can make a crafting table using the logs they have obtained.

The crafting table is used to make basic tools like a wooden pickaxe needed to mine blocks such as cobblestone and coal. Cobblestone can be obtained from mining stone blocks which can be used to make a furnace. A furnace is neccessary in order to cook food and smelt any ore. A pickaxe is especially useful in order to obtain coal that a player has mined. Coal is a useful item that can be used to make food and torches which provides lighting.

 

Night Time Dangers

During the night, the primary dangers would be hostile mobs that spawn only during the dark. Here, I list some of the most commonly seen mobs in the game.

  • Zombies
  • Skeletons
  • Creepers
  • Spiders

There are many efficient ways to kill a mob however, a sword is the most commonly picked method. If a player waits a certain amount of time between attacks with their weapon, they would be able to deal bonus damage in comparison if they attacked a target without any delay between attacks. Although killing a monster may be thrilling to do, many players die due to a lack of experience. What they should do is build a shelter and wait out the night as monsters will die from the sunlight during the day time.

 

Shelter

Before a player's first night, they should make a shelter of any block in order to secure a safe home away from danger. This allows a player to avoid dangers and a higher chance of not being killed by a monster. Potential homes would depend on the landscape. For instance a flat space would be an ideal location so players would be able to

  • Have a good view of the surrounding area
  • Open space for decorations
  • Fence around house for extra protection

A bed is an item that a player should aim to get as it is used to skip the night time and wake up during the day. It also sets the spawn point if a player were to die.

 

Food and Hunger

After making tools and a shelter, a player's next objective would be to find food. Hunger level decreases as a player moves around. When the hunger bar drops below 90%, the player will not regenerate health. If a player is below 30%, the player will not be able to sprint. If the hunger bar becomes empty, players will slowly lose health. Hunger level is drained rapidly when a player:

  • Attacking monsters and receiving damage
  • Sprinting by double-tapping the forward w key.
  • Jumping
  • Swimming and mining blocks

Food is neccessary to fill up your hunger bar. Having a basic crop farm to make bread or breeding animals and killing them to get meat is necessary for survival.

 

Definitions

Log
Block found in trees which are primarily used to create planks. A log comes in six different species being oak, birch, jungle, spruce, acacia, and dark oak.
Crafting Table
Used by players to craft a variety of blocks and items.
Pickaxe
The most commonly used tools in the game to mine all ores, rock and metal-based blocks. Depending on the material of the pick, it allows players to mine in different speeds.
Furnace
Used to smelt blocks and items and to convert them into other blocks or items.
Torch
Blocks that emit light.
Bed
Allows players to sleep and reset their spawn point.
Hunger
Regulates player's health regeneration and ability to sprint.
